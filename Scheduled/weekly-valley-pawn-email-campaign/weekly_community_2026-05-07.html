Twelve Years.
One Promise.

A Mother's Day note from the family behind the counter.
 

Some things you can't appraise on a scale.

When we opened our first store in 2014, our mission was simple: treat every person who walks in the way we'd want our own family treated. Twelve years later, that hasn't changed one bit.

A lot of you have stories with us. The wedding ring you needed back. The tools that got you through a tough month. The graduation gift you found in our case. We don't take a single one of those moments lightly.

This Mother's Day weekend, we're thinking about the moms — ours, yours, and the customers we've come to know by name — who taught us that doing what's right matters more than doing what's easy. That's a lesson we carry into every appraisal, every loan, every sale.

"What's Right Is Right."

Three words. Twelve years. Five stores. One family.

Looking for a Mother's Day gift?

Stop in this week. Our cases are full of one-of-a-kind jewelry, fine watches, and pieces with real character. Everything we sell comes with our 30-day warranty, and our team will help you find something she'll actually love, not just unwrap.

No pressure, no upsell. Just honest help from people who know what they're doing.
