Pawn Loans 101

How a pawn loan actually works

No credit check. No hit to your score. Just a fair, modern look at your stuff — from people who’ve been doing this in the Valley since 2014.

The 90-second version

A pawn loan is a short-term loan secured by something you already own. You bring it in, we appraise it, and you walk out with cash the same day. When you’re ready to pay it back, your item comes home with you. That’s the whole idea.

Most folks are surprised by how simple it is — and how much it doesn’t look like the movies.

1

Bring in something of value

Jewelry, tools, electronics, instruments, watches — if it has resale value, we’ll take a look. No appointment needed.
2

We appraise it fairly

We use real-time data and app-driven tools — not a gut feeling — to give you an honest number. If you don’t love the offer, you walk out with your item. No pressure, no judgment.
3

Walk out with cash the same day

No credit check. No application. No effect on your credit score — ever. Your item is held safely while the loan is active.
4

Pay it back — your item comes home

You have 30 days, with extensions available. Pay in person, by phone, or right from our mobile app. Done — it goes back in your hands.

And if you’d rather sell? We buy outright too — gold, silver, electronics, tools, the works. Either way, everything we resell comes with our standard 30-day warranty. That’s our promise: what’s right is right.
