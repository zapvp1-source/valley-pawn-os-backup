## What this tells us (Roanoke — likely echoes across all 5)

Three high-leverage gaps, all fixable, all pointing the same direction:

1. **No Store subscription** → overpaying final-value fees. At ~$40K/yr in sales, a Basic ($21.95/mo) or Premium ($59.95/mo) Store lowers FVF enough to more than cover its cost, and adds free listings + promotion tools. **Cost win.**
2. **Promoted Listings switched off** → 11,157 views but only 92 orders. Promoted Listings (pay only when it sells) is the single biggest visibility lever and it's at zero. A 30%-off promo is expiring 6/30. **Sales win.**
3. **Not Top Rated** → missing the FVF discount + search badge. Two metrics block it: defect rate (4 defects) and unresolved cases (2), plus weak tracking-upload (76.74%). Fixing handling/tracking discipline and resolving cases before they close against us moves all three. **Cost + reputation win.**

Underneath those: 68 listings (34%) have interested buyers eligible for offers, and 1 offer is sitting unanswered — quick revenue with no spend.
