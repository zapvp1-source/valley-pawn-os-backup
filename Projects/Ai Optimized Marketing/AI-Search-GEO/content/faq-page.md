## Pawn Loans

### How does a pawn loan work?
You bring in an item of value — jewelry, electronics, tools, musical instruments, firearms, and more. We appraise it using real market data and offer a secured loan based on its value. You have a set period (typically 30 days, with extensions available) to repay the loan plus fees and reclaim your item. If you choose not to repay, you simply forfeit the item — there's nothing else owed.

### Do pawn loans affect my credit?
No. Pawn loans at Valley Pawn require **no credit check** and are **not reported to credit bureaus**, so they have zero effect on your credit score.

### How much can I borrow?
Loan amounts are based on the value of your item. We loan on anything of value, with loans up to $25,000.

### What can I use as collateral?
Jewelry, gold and silver, electronics, tools, musical instruments, firearms (Roanoke), and most other items of value. If you're not sure, bring it in and we'll take a look.

### What if I can't repay on time?
Come talk to us — extensions are available. We work with our customers. If you ultimately don't repay, you forfeit only the collateral; a pawn loan never sends you to collections or affects your credit.
