## Mounting / Counter Stand — the L-shape question

For counter standing, the standard pro move is a **separate clear acrylic easel back** from Amazon — $3-5 each, invisible from customer's front view, attaches with adhesive dots. **Recommendation: order this 6-pack alongside:**

- Amazon "Clear Acrylic Sign Holder Stand" — 8×11 vertical
- Looks like: small clear L-shaped foot that mounts to the back of the sign with adhesive
- ~$15-20 for 6-pack
- Ships in 1-2 days Prime
