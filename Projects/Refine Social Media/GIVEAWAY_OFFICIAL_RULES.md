# Valley Pawn $100 Monthly Giveaway — Official Rules

**Effective:** 2026-07-01 (recurring monthly thereafter until canceled)
**Sponsor:** Full Circle Finance Inc DBA Valley Pawn, 5 Virginia locations (Lexington, Roanoke, Harrisonburg, Waynesboro, Culpeper)

> **NO PURCHASE OR PAYMENT OF ANY KIND IS NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN.** A purchase will not increase your chances of winning. Void where prohibited.

---

## 1. Eligibility
Open only to legal residents of the Commonwealth of Virginia who are **18 years of age or older** at time of entry. Employees of Full Circle Finance Inc, their immediate family members (spouse, parents, siblings, children), and members of the same household are not eligible. By entering, you confirm you meet these requirements.

## 2. Entry Period
Each monthly drawing has an entry period of one (1) calendar month. Entries received between **12:00 AM ET on the 1st** and **11:59 PM ET on the last day** of a given month are eligible for that month's drawing. Entries received after 11:59 PM ET on the last day roll into the following month.

## 3. How to Enter
**One (1) free method of entry per person per month, no purchase necessary:**

Visit **follow.thevalleypawn.com** and submit your email address through the entry form. You will receive one (1) entry into the drawing for the current month.

**Alternate mail-in entry** (also free, no purchase necessary): Print or hand-write your full name, address, phone number, email address, and date of birth on a 3"×5" card and mail to *Valley Pawn Giveaway, 571 James Madison Highway, Culpeper, VA 22701*. Entries must be postmarked by the last day of the entry period and received within five (5) business days thereafter. Limit one mail-in entry per envelope.

**Limit:** One (1) entry per person per monthly entry period, regardless of entry method.

## 4. Drawing
One (1) winner per monthly entry period will be selected at random from all eligible entries received during that period. The drawing will occur within five (5) business days following the end of the entry period. Odds of winning depend on the total number of eligible entries received.

## 5. Prize
One Hundred Dollars ($100) USD, awarded as a Valley Pawn store credit redeemable at any of the five (5) Virginia locations OR a $100 prepaid Visa gift card at the winner's option. Approximate Retail Value (ARV): $100. Limit one (1) prize per winner per twelve (12) month period.

## 6. Winner Notification
The winner will be notified by email at the address provided in their entry, and announced publicly on Valley Pawn's social media channels (Facebook, Instagram, X, TikTok, YouTube). Winner must respond to the notification email within seven (7) days to claim the prize, or an alternate winner will be selected at random. Prize must be claimed in person at a Valley Pawn location within sixty (60) days of notification, with valid government-issued photo ID matching the entry name.

## 7. Taxes
Each prize is valued under $600. Winner is responsible for any applicable taxes. No 1099 form will be issued for prizes under the IRS reporting threshold.
