# Valley Pawn — 30-Day Content Calendar (Brand + 5 Store Pages + @valley_pawn IG)

**Window:** Sunday June 22 - Sunday July 20, 2026
**Posting cadence:** Daily across the brand + rotating store-local
**Strategy:** Hybrid — Brand content cross-posts to FB Brand + @valley_pawn IG. Store-local content goes only to that store's FB Page.

---

## CONTENT PILLARS (the categories that drive everything)

| Pillar | What it is | Cadence | Audience |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Gold Buy** | Educational + promotional — "we buy gold and silver, here's why now" | 2x/week brand | Brand IG + Brand FB |
| **New Arrivals** | Showcase recent intake (tools, jewelry, electronics, instruments) | 3x/week brand + 1x/week per store | Brand IG + Brand FB + relevant store FB |
| **Customer Stories** | Real customers who got a fair deal — testimonials, reviews | 1x/week brand | Brand IG + Brand FB |
| **Store Spotlight** | Behind-the-scenes at a specific location, team intro, local event | 1x/week rotating | That store's FB Page only |
| **Holiday / Seasonal** | Major holidays + Valley Pawn-specific seasonal pushes (back-to-school, hunting season, etc.) | Around the date | Brand IG + Brand FB + all store FBs |
| **Education** | "How pawn loans work" / "30-day warranty explained" / "Why we don't take stolen goods" | 1x/week brand | Brand IG + Brand FB |
| **Operations** | Store hours, closed days, new payment methods, weather closures | As needed | Affected store FB |

1. **vp-content-batch generates the week's content every Sunday at 8pm** — pulls fresh Bravo new-arrivals data, applies pillars to suggested posts
2. **Pushes content into Publer's queue via Publer's API** — auto-scheduled at the times specified above
3. **Joshua reviews dashboard Sunday night or Monday morning** — 10 min — approves/edits anything that needs it
4. **Publer auto-publishes throughout the week**
5. **friday_close_engagement.py reads metrics every Friday at 5pm** — produces engagement report with reactions/comments/shares per post
6. **Following Sunday: review last week's performance, generate next week**
